Banana Chocolate Milkshake

Preparation Time:10mins

Serving:10 glasses

  • 5 ripe bananas
  • 5 cups chilled milk
  • 5 tbsp cocoa powder (or use chocolate syrup)
  • 5 tbsp sugar or honey (adjust to taste)
  • 10 ice cubes
  • Ice cream, grated chocolate
  1. Peel and slice bananas.
  2. Add bananas, cocoa powder, sugar, and milk to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  5. Pour into 10 glasses.
  6. Top with grated chocolate or a scoop of ice cream.
